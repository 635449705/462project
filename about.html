About us

Products and Services

JWCBooks today is a premier online retailer of new, used and electronic textbooks for sale or rent, study materials and book buyback services headquartered in dekalb, IL. In addition to direct retail sales via the online JWCBooks storefront, the company partners with colleges, universities and K-12 schools to serve as their official bookstore. Currently the JWCBooks Virtual Bookstore Program serves as the official bookstore for over 150 schools nationwide and that number continues to grow each year.

Our Philosophy

With students best interests at the core of all efforts put forth by JWCBooks, the company has an "Adapt or Die" philosophy, making necessary changes to their business model to best suit the modern day student. The company's executive team is comprised of the former Vice President, Director of Customer Service, Distribution Operations Manager and Senior Project Manager at Thomson Learning, which is known today as Cengage Learning. This invaluable experience and the company's winning philosophy have allowed JWCBooks to remain profitable today, where other online textbook industry leaders are not.
